About

My name is Justin, and I am a software developer.

I grew up in Vancouver, leaving for Montreal to study Mathematics and Computer Science at McGill University. Though I now spend my days engaged in the practicalities of software engineering, my time at McGill studying pure mathematics gave me an appreciation for and training in the rigorous standards of proof, logical argumentation, and abstract analysis of the subject, which still find good use daily in my problem solving process.

After graduating from McGill, I returned to Vancouver as a software developer. Working for a year afforded me the chance to marry the love of my life and start my family. After a couple years in a developer role, I transitioned to team lead and, most recently, team manager positions.

I enjoy the challenges and opportunities that come with the ever-changing landscape of software, and exploring how the latest technologies can be combined with proven methodologies to build powerful and effective systems.

When not developing software, I enjoy spending time with my lovely wife and children.
